FAQ Section About Mercedes G63

Powerful drive unit At the heart of the Mercedes-AMG G 63 is the legendary 4.0-litre V8 biturbo engine with 430 kW (585 hp) and 850 Nm of torque. This has been coupled with a starter generator for the first time and boasts even more explosive power delivery and breathtaking torque.

Gelandewagen Ever wondered what the “G” in the G-Class stands for? It surely isn't just a letter that Mercedes-Benz assigns to different models in its lineup. It stands for “Gelandewagen”, which means cross country vehicle. This is why it is also commonly known as simply G-wagen, which is just a short form of the name.
